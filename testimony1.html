Postrate Enlargement Healed

Praise the Lord! E yin Oluwa logo!!! My name is Bro David Oladipo from Orozo Region, Abundant Life Group, Hope District.


In April, when our father in the Lord came, I was diagnosed of prostate enlargement and it was so confirmed.


Before then, I’ve been on drugs but I just determined that that April, as soon as I set my eyes on our father in the Lord, this thing must stop. That time, I usually urinate up to five times before daybreak and I will not be able to sleep.


But to the glory of God, on that day, as soon Baba stepped in and he was introduced, I felt uneasy in me. Immediately, I went to toilet. After the service, I went back home. That particular night, I want to tell you that I did not even urinate once till daybreak.


The following day, I went to the hospital to confirm what has happened to me. Alas, when I was tested, they said that the enlargement is gone and my prostate is very normal. Praise the Lord!
